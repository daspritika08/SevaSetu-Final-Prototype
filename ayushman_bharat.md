Scheme Name: Ayushman Bharat - PM Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY)

Objective: World’s largest health insurance scheme for secondary and tertiary care.

Benefit: * Health cover of ₹5 Lakh per family per year.

Cashless and paperless access to services at empanelled hospitals.

Special 2026 Feature: Universal coverage for all senior citizens aged 70+ regardless of income.

Eligibility: Bottom 40% of the population (vulnerable families), landless laborers, and SC/ST households.
