Scheme Name: Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

Objective: Enhance livelihood security by providing at least 100 days of guaranteed wage employment in a financial year to every rural household.

Benefit: Daily wage employment within 15 days of application. If not provided, an unemployment allowance is paid.

Eligibility: * Adult members of a rural household willing to do unskilled manual work.

Must possess a valid Job Card.

Documents Needed: Aadhaar card, Bank/Post Office passbook, and a passport-size photograph.
