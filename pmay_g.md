Scheme Name: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G)

Objective: "Housing for All"—providing a permanent house with basic amenities to homeless families.

Benefit: * Plains: Financial assistance of ₹1.20 Lakh.

Hilly/Difficult Areas: ₹1.30 Lakh.

Additional: ₹12,000 for toilet construction via Swachh Bharat Mission.

House Specs: Minimum size of 25 sq. mt. with a dedicated hygienic cooking area.

Eligibility: Homeless families or those living in kutcha houses (0, 1, or 2 rooms) as per SECC data.
